Living on another planet?

The ideal is not to go too far, on a planet resembling the Earth, with an atmosphere to protect us from radiation and meteorites, a relatively regulated temperature, endowed with ice or liquid water, with days and nights, and if possible rocky. Mars is our closest neighbour, it takes about a year and a half to get there, but it doesn't meet all of these criteria. Proxima b would be a more directly habitable option… But this planet is located outside the solar system and it would take at least 100,000 years to reach it!

Save the Planet: Vertical Farms

The world is currently concerned by a major challenge. Today, we are 7 billion on Earth and the figure will be estimated at 9 billion in 2050. An inevitable question arises: what solutions exist to feed all humanity? As we have just seen, razing forests to plant fields there would be a disaster for biodiversity. This is why the “vertical farms” project was devised. Recently, 7x7 gave you 7 good reasons to start urban agriculture. The "vertical farms" are these uninhabited buildings whose function is to house crops of all kinds, whether rice fields, orchards or even vegetable gardens or wheat fields. This project is part of the legitimate need to double food production. If the plants will grow here above ground, these vertical farms will have the advantage of the absence of the use of pesticides, the crops being protected from insects and bacteria. Finally, given that 80% of the world's population will live in cities in 2050, these vertical farms should make it possible to considerably reduce transport time between cities and the countryside. So many advantages, which countries like China, Japan, Singapore and the United States have noticed. It is in the latter country, in Chicago, that the first vertical farm in the world was set up in 2010. In Japan, nearly 300 projects are underway. In Singapore, a city-state with very little arable land, the company Sky Greens has set up 550 vertical farms, each 9 meters high. The future has already begun.
